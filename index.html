// How We Work

Simple Process.
Serious Results.

01

Discovery Call (30 min)

We map your problem, your users, and what "done" looks like. I ask the uncomfortable questions early so they don't become expensive surprises at week six. No NDAs needed to talk.

02

Proposal & Scope

You get a clear document: what gets built, when it's done, and what it costs. Fixed-price for defined scopes. Weekly retainer for ongoing work. No retainer-to-disappear billing.

03

Build in Public

Weekly async video updates. Staging environment from day one so you can see real progress, not screenshots. You're never waiting to find out what I've been doing.

04

Ship & Hand Off

Full documentation, clean git history, and a recorded walkthrough. You own the code entirely. I stay on for 30 days post-launch to catch any edge cases your real users reveal.

A Dev Who's

Six years ago I was the guy who built "technically impressive" features nobody used. That changed after watching a startup I'd built most of the tech for collapse — not because the code was bad, but because we'd optimized for the wrong metrics entirely.

Now I ask "why" before "how." Every architecture decision maps back to a business outcome. Speed of delivery matters, but not more than building the right thing. I'm not an order-taker — I'm the developer who tells you when your idea has a faster path to the same goal.

Based remotely. Work with clients in Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia. Native English. Available for full project ownership or senior-level augmentation on existing teams.
